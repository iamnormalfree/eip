🌐 Big Picture: What This Framework Is

This framework is basically a “content brain” for your company.
It learns how you think, how your brand speaks, and how your industry works — then produces publish-ready material (articles, guides, landing pages, or explanations) that are fact-checked, compliant, and strategically sound.

Think of it as:

A miniature editorial team made of AI agents — each with a defined role (planner, writer, editor, auditor, legal checker, and brand stylist).

🧩 The Core Idea

Most people use AI like a fast typewriter.
You’re building something very different — an educational IP engine that teaches the AI how to think like you, not just what to write.

It doesn’t just “generate content.”
It runs through a structured process that mirrors how top consulting firms or educators develop thought leadership:

Define a framework of thinking (Educational IP)

Find reliable evidence

Generate arguments and examples

Self-check and repair

Ensure compliance

Publish with full provenance

So the system becomes your institutional brain, not just a writing tool.

📚 “Educational IP”: The Secret Sauce

Instead of one-size-fits-all prompts, the system uses what you called Educational IPs — reusable “thinking grammars” for specific types of writing.

Examples:

Framework IP: explains why something works (cause → effect).

Process IP: walks through how to do something step by step.

Comparative IP: helps people choose between options.

Narrative IP: teaches through story and moral.

Policy IP: defines rules and boundaries.

Data-Driven IP: explains insights from real numbers.

Each piece of content is built on one of these IPs — so every output has structure and logic, not just words.

🧱 The Data Backbone

Under the hood, it keeps three kinds of data in sync:

Supabase database (truth):
Stores structured knowledge — your frameworks, evidence, brand rules, etc.

Graph database (memory):
Links concepts (“Refinancing → LTV → MAS rules → Case studies”) so it can reason about relationships, not just keywords.

Obsidian vault (human-friendly view):
A synced folder where you can see and edit the material in plain English — the “living library.”

This means your ideas, data, and tone are captured once and reused across all future content.

🔍 Retrieval: How It Finds the Right Stuff

When you ask it to write something, it doesn’t go straight to Google or guess.
It runs a three-channel search:

Graph search: related concepts in your own knowledge base

Keyword search (BM25): finds exact matches in your text library

Vector hints: finds analogies (“refinancing → restructuring”)

Then it merges results with weights and filters out irrelevant ones.
If your internal data is sparse, it automatically falls back to the keyword route and tags the result as #GRAPH_SPARSE so you know it relied more on search than structured knowledge.

🧮 Self-Verification (“Response Awareness”)

Every draft runs through two independent checks:

Micro-Auditor:
An AI editor that checks for 10 high-value failure points:

Missing mechanism

No counterexample

Weak or missing evidence

Legal/compliance issue

Off-persona tone

Broken CTA, etc.

Repairer:
A tiny AI that edits only the problem section (not the whole piece).
It’s like a sub-editor fixing specific paragraphs.

This gives you a near-zero hallucination rate and dramatically reduces manual cleanup.

⚖️ Compliance & Governance

All sources are allow-listed (e.g. MAS, IRAS, .gov, .edu).

Financial claims must link to verifiable data or be rewritten as “conditional.”

JSON-LD and legal disclaimers are rendered from approved templates, never invented by the model.

You get a traceable ledger for every piece — what sources were used, when last checked, and who approved it.

So you can show regulators how every claim was generated.

🧍 Human-in-the-Loop (The “5% Vibe Check” Done Right)

Instead of proofreading everything, you or your editors score each draft on:

Tone (authentic to brand?)

Strategic fit

Novelty

Compliance

These scores update the brand DNA so the system learns over time.
It literally evolves your taste into code.

💰 Cost & Control

Every piece has a strict budget — token and time limits per tier:

Light tier for simple or internal pages.

Medium tier for publish-ready articles.

Heavy tier for regulatory or flagship content.

If a piece exceeds limits, it stops and flags the job — no silent overruns.
If the AI gets stuck, it goes into a dead-letter queue for manual review.

This keeps quality high and costs predictable.

🪄 What Happens When You Hit “Go”

When you trigger a job:

It loads your brand profile (tone + rules).

It analyzes the brief and picks the right Educational IP.

It gathers the right sources.

It writes, checks, and repairs itself.

It builds a publish-ready draft (with sections, data, and CTAs).

It adds links and JSON-LD for SEO.

It records everything in a ledger (for auditing).

You get a review interface to approve or tweak.

After approval, it updates the brand DNA and improves next time.
